Using AI to find phenotypes of the mitochondria of senescent cells

We are using CNNs, Phenotypic profiling and Autoencoders to analyze the mitochondria of senescent (zombie-type cells) vs. proliferating cells in the hope of finding a consistent phenotype for replicative senescence.

    • Determining the effect of senolytics on the epigenome of genes associated with senescence
